THESIS / RESEARCH — 2026

Glenoid Baseplate Micromotion in Shoulder Implants

An engineering thesis examining how screw orientation angle affects micromotion at the baseplate–bone interface under cyclic loading — a key factor in whether an implant achieves long-term osseointegration or loosens over time.

  • FocusShoulder arthroplasty, implant fixation
  • MethodCyclic loading tests, micromotion measurement
  • VariableScrew orientation angle
